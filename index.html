The Journey

My Story

The Beginning: My journey into the digital world didn't start with drag-and-drop tools; it started with raw code. I spent years mastering the logic behind Backend Architecture and Database Management. This deep technical foundation allows me to solve complex problems that regular designers can't handle.


The Evolution: While I loved coding, I realized that modern businesses need speed and agility. That’s why I expanded my expertise to include Shopify, WordPress, and Wix. I combine my coding skills with these platforms to create custom, high-performance stores that are easy for my clients to manage.


The Mission: I noticed a gap in the market: developers who could code didn't understand marketing, and marketers didn't understand code. To bridge this, I founded Equafusion.com. My goal is to provide a complete ecosystem—Development, Design, and Ads—so you don't have to hire multiple agencies.


My Promise: When you work with me, you are not just a transaction. I believe in building long-term partnerships. That is why I offer 24/7 Support to ensure your business never faces downtime.
